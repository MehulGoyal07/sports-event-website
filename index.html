Information about the Event

By joining our marathon, you not only challenge yourself with a rewarding run but also gain access to exclusive perks. Enjoy a custom event t-shirt, a personalized race medal, and Refreshments spot every 800mtr.

Event Details

Get ready for an exciting marathon event on 25th August 2024, starting at 6:00 AM in the vibrant locale of Wagholi, Maharashtra. Mark this date in your calendar and join us for a memorable day of fitness and fun. The event promises a lively atmosphere and a well-organized experience, ensuring you have an unforgettable time.

Participation Information

Our marathon is open to everyone, with specific categories for men, women, children, and senior citizens. Registration is just ₹1, and you can easily sign up through our website by providing your name, age, contact details, and preferred category. Ensure you register by 25th August 2024 to secure your spot. Don’t miss the chance to be part of this exciting event!

Participant Benefits

As a participant, you’ll enjoy a range of exclusive benefits, including a free t-shirt for the first 2000 registrants and an OTG pen drive. Along the route, we’ll provide refreshing water stations, lemonade, glucose drinks, and snacks. Your safety is our priority with ambulances, medical points, and portable toilets available. Plus, immerse yourself in the fun with Zumba sessions, Arabic performances, and exciting prize distributions for winners. Celebrate your achievement with us and make lasting memories!
